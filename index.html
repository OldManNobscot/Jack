About Me

Driven, Reliable & Boston Proud

I'm Jack Berry, a graduating senior from Framingham High School with a passion for leadership, creativity, and community. Whether I'm running a Model U.N. conference, supervising campers at Drumlin Farm, or supporting special education students as a Teacher's Aide, I show up fully committed and ready to contribute.

My Eagle Scout rank — the highest achievement in Scouting — represents years of perseverance, service, and character. I completed the Boy Scout National Youth Leadership Training program, and I bring those leadership skills into every classroom, club, and community effort I'm a part of.

I'm a proud Framingham native with deep Boston roots. Red Sox games, local volunteering, and connecting with my community are core parts of who I am.
